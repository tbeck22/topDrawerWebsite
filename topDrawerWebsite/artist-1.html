About Ben Palmer

After seeing the work of Maullin and Friedlander, Ben picked up a 35mm camera and began shooting around London and the world. He was offered a photojournalism scholarship at London Communication College where he built up an impressive portfolio, showing work in private shows and exhibitions such as photocopy club. Bens currently working on a book and putting a new body of work together for a solo show. His raw style of photography shows as for the past year or so he has been processing, scanning and printing everything by hand.
